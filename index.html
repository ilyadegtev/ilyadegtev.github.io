About me

I am a condensed matter physicist. I received my PhD in Physics from Sapienza University of Rome in 2025, where I investigated the structural and electronic properties of silver fluorides as analogues of cuprate superconductors. My research combines scanning tunnelling microscopy (STM), X-ray spectroscopy, surface science, and first-principles calculations to study quantum materials and strongly correlated electron systems.

Research interests

  • Quantum materials
  • Strongly correlated electron systems
  • Scanning tunnelling microscopy (STM)
  • X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAS)
  • Resonant inelastic X-ray scattering (RIXS)
  • Surface science
  • Silver fluorides and cuprate analogues
  • Density-functional theory (DFT)
